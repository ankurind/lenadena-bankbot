# LenaDena Bank — Policies & Guidelines

## 1. Account Closure Policy

**1.1 Eligibility for Closure**
Any LenaDena Bank account holder may request account closure at any time, subject to the following conditions:
- No outstanding dues (overdraft, loan EMI, credit card balance) linked to the account.
- All pending transactions must be settled.
- Demat accounts linked to the savings account must be closed separately.

**1.2 Closure Fees**
- Accounts closed within 12 months of opening: ₹300 closure fee.
- Accounts open for more than 12 months: No closure fee.
- Tax-saving FD accounts cannot be closed before 5-year lock-in period.

**1.3 Process**
- Account holder must visit the home branch in person with a written closure request, passbook, and debit card (cut/surrendered).
- Proceeds are paid by cheque (account payee) or NEFT to a third-party account.
- Processing time: 3–5 working days.
- Joint account closure requires signatures from all joint holders.

**1.4 Dormant Accounts**
- Accounts with no transactions for 12 months are classified as dormant.
- Dormant accounts are re-activated by visiting the branch with photo ID.
- Accounts dormant for 10+ years are transferred to the RBI Depositor Education and Awareness Fund (DEAF).

## 2. Dispute Resolution Policy

**2.1 Scope**
This policy covers disputes related to: unauthorized transactions, incorrect debit/credit, ATM cash dispense failures, failed online transactions, and billing errors on credit cards.

**2.2 Reporting Timeline**
- Disputes must be reported within 30 days of the transaction date for credit cards.
- For savings/current account disputes: within 60 days.
- ATM cash dispense failures: within 10 days (as per RBI guidelines).

**2.3 Resolution Process**
| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Customer raises dispute via helpline, net banking, or branch | Day 0 |
| 2 | Acknowledgement sent to customer via SMS/email | Within 2 business days |
| 3 | Provisional reversal (for unauthorized transactions above ₹5,000) | Within 5 business days |
| 4 | Investigation and root cause determination | 7–21 working days |
| 5 | Resolution communicated and final adjustment made | Within 21 working days |

**2.4 Escalation**
- Level 1: Branch Manager
- Level 2: Grievance Officer — grievance.officer@lenadenbank.in
- Level 3: Banking Ombudsman (RBI) — available after 30 days if unresolved internally

**2.5 Liability Framework (per RBI circular)**
- Zero liability if reported within 3 working days.
- Limited liability (max ₹10,000) if reported between 4–7 working days.
- Customer bears full liability if reported after 7 working days and negligence is proven.

## 3. Loan Eligibility & Lending Policy

**3.1 General Principles**
LenaDena Bank's lending decisions are based on: creditworthiness (CIBIL score), repayment capacity (debt-to-income ratio), collateral (where applicable), and banking relationship history.

**3.2 Credit Score Requirements**
| Loan Type | Minimum CIBIL Score |
|-----------|---------------------|
| Home Loan | 700 |
| Personal Loan | 700 |
| Car Loan (New) | 680 |
| Car Loan (Used) | 700 |
| Education Loan | 650 (co-applicant) |
| Gold Loan | No minimum |
| Loan Against Property | 680 |

**3.3 Debt-to-Income (DTI) Ratio**
- LenaDena Bank follows RBI guidelines: total EMI obligations (including the proposed loan) must not exceed 50% of gross monthly income.
- Example: If monthly income is ₹60,000, total EMIs (existing + new) must be ≤ ₹30,000.

**3.4 Loan Rejection & Communication**
- Loan applications are decided within 7–10 working days from submission of complete documents.
- Rejection reasons are communicated in writing.
- Customers may reapply after 6 months.
- LenaDena Bank does not guarantee loan approval to any applicant. Meeting eligibility criteria is a necessary but not sufficient condition.

**3.5 Pre-payment Policy**
| Loan Type | Pre-payment Penalty |
|-----------|---------------------|
| Home Loan (floating) | Nil |
| Personal Loan (after 12 EMIs) | 2% of outstanding |
| Personal Loan (after 36 EMIs) | Nil |
| Car Loan | 2% of outstanding |
| Education Loan | Nil after moratorium ends |
| Gold Loan | Nil |

## 4. KYC & Privacy Policy

**4.1 KYC Requirements**
LenaDena Bank complies with RBI KYC guidelines (Master Direction 2016). Full KYC must be completed within 12 months of account opening. Accounts on minimum KYC are restricted to ₹50,000 balance and ₹1 lakh annual transactions.

**4.2 Periodic KYC Update**
- High-risk customers: every 2 years
- Medium-risk customers: every 8 years
- Low-risk customers: every 10 years
Failure to update KYC results in account restriction.

**4.3 Data Privacy**
LenaDena Bank does not share personal customer data with third parties except:
- As required by law or regulatory mandate
- With credit bureaus (CIBIL, Experian) for credit reporting
- With partner insurers (with explicit customer consent)
All data is stored in India and protected under the IT Act 2000 and DPDP Act 2023.

**4.4 What We Never Ask**
LenaDena Bank will NEVER ask customers for:
- OTPs, PINs, or passwords over phone, SMS, or email
- Net banking login credentials
- Card CVV number

Report any such requests immediately to 1800-123-5362.

## 5. Interest Rate Policy

**5.1 Savings Account Interest**
Interest is calculated on daily closing balance and credited on the last working day of every quarter.

**5.2 FD Interest**
FD interest rate is fixed at booking. Rate changes do not affect existing FDs.

**5.3 Loan Interest**
- Floating rate loans are linked to LenaDena Bank's Repo Linked Lending Rate (RLLR).
- RLLR is reviewed quarterly and changes are passed on from the next EMI cycle.
- Fixed rate loans are locked for the agreed tenure.

**5.4 Rate Transparency**
Current rates are always displayed at all LenaDena Bank branches and on the official website. Verbal rate commitments by staff are not binding — only the written sanction letter is authoritative.
